Reflection

My experience with the WEB222 course was excellent. In fact, it is my favourite course among all of them. This is due to multiple factors, but especially the professor. His method of teaching is very effective, and he is also very approachable and ready to help if someone has difficulties. The classes were interactive, and the professor was supportive and encouraged us to participate.

In this course, we started by learning the basics of the internet and how it works, and then started learning JavaScript. This part was quite easy because I already knew the C language from the previous semester. Then we learnt HTML and CSS. This was my favourite part because I always loved design. The assignments were helpful because they were designed to strengthen what we learned in the classroom.

In my opinion, these concepts will help me a lot in the future because I want to be a web designer.
